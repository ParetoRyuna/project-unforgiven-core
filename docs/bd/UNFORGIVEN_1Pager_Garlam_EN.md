UNFORGIVEN v2

> March 2026 | Jiani Zhao

## Why now
High-traffic Solana drops still break the same way. Bots and Sybil wallets win in milliseconds. Real users lose access. Teams lose trust.

## What it is
UNFORGIVEN v2 is a fairness layer for ticketing, launchpads, and gated access on Solana. It sits between user requests and app logic, then returns a clear path: allow, step_up, or block.

## What is already working
- end-to-end fairness flow on Solana
- integration contract and plugin path
- reproducible tests and smoke demos
- behavior-based shadow scoring feeding the decision layer

## What comes next
The next step is one real pilot. The best fit is ticketing, launchpads, or gated access. The goal is tight scope, real traffic, and fast iteration.

## What would help
1-2 intros to teams that can evaluate this in a real pilot or technical validation.

Current focus: pilot validation and distribution.
